Change Log 05.07.09

Change Log for Thursday, May 7, 2009

I picked up the plumbing pieces for the master bathroom shower and got some miscellaneous things at the hardware store.  I tried to order the counter for the master bathroom, but found that the person who had given me the quote did it incorrectly and the real cost is more than double the original quote.  So, we’re back to square one on the master bathroom counter.  I picked up some formica samples, but I didn’t find anything particularly appealing.  We may do sealed plywood temporarily and then do a concrete countertop in there after all.

The tile guys finished working on the master bathroom and it looks great.  The downstairs bath is all ready to go once the tile comes in.  They should be back on Thursday of next week to finish up that bathroom and will probably be done on Friday.  I am so glad we subbed all this out, even if it is costing a fair amount.  Tile is a lot of work, and it is very important that it is done correctly! 

I went down to the house to work on the flooring in the afternoon, but Nathan didn’t nap like he usually does so I ended up not being able to do it.  I’m hoping to get to work on it some more this afternoon if I can get Nathan to do his usual nap.
